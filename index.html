Campus life

TARQUIE PUBLIC SCHOOL-CAMPUS

Tarquie Public School is a Central board of Education located in America. The pre-primary and early years learning programme at CPS is a system where the tenets of Montessori education are scientifically combined with tried and tested teaching methods, focussing on language development and logical thinking, for children to start their first steps into the education system.

ART CLASSROOM

To kindle a conducive learning environment on par with the best institutions worldwide, Tarquie Public School has spared no effort or investment to ensure that the best infrastructure is made available to students to enable them to pursue their education with ease and comfort.

Smart Class – Interactive Digital classroom:

Tarquie Public School was one of the first schools in India to have smart boards in all the classrooms and also a 5.1 theatre system in all classrooms.

Center of excellence for kids

ATAL tinkering labs take CPS one step closer to the vision ‘Cultivate one Million children in India as Neoteric Innovators’.They are the pivots of innovation that foster imagination and creativity in young minds.

Sports and games
Sports and games

Students are segregated into four houses namely Red House, Blue House, Yellow House and Green House. Intra-mural games and sports are conducted to promote healthy competitions among the four houses where the adrenaline is gushed up as they take part in all events energetically.
